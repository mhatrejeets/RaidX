About RaidX

RaidX is a digital Kabaddi platform for live Kabaddi scoring, live score streaming, and match updates. We help players, organizers, owners, and viewers stay connected to every raid.

From local tournaments to big events, RaidX brings live Kabaddi action, player insights, and clear score tracking in one place.
